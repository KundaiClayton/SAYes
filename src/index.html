What does SAYes Mentoring do?

We design, deliver and support mentorship programmes, connecting independent volunteers and business team(mentors) with young people (mentees) to support possitive life outcomes.

What does a mentor do?

Mentors guide young people to develop a deep vision for their lives, helping them to set personal and proffesional goals supporting healthy growth in every are that matters

Are you a young person?

If you`re ready to change your life, and improve the quality of your decision making in every area, Apply for a mentor now(free)

Why mentorship is one of the best vehicles for social change.

Most change fails because it fails to consider howpowerfully social human beings are. We have foung that the best way to create lasting change is through relationships.

When high value relationships are structured and supported, it profoundly accelerates skills, values and knowledge in every area of life.

Since 2008, we have used structured and supported mentorships to improve life outcomes for thousands of young people.

How can high quality mentorship be free?


We all know that if something is free, there's usually a catch.We're part of a new vision for philanthropy, a growing global community committed to generosity at scale, to fund massive social change, focused on the world's most pressing problems.

We are inspiring businesses, governments, and high net-worth individuals to make social change investments. This allows us to offer world-class training and support to Mentors and Mentees, totally free.

With new Mentors and new Mentees signing up everyday, our aim is to usher in a new era of mentorship - where valuable life lessons can be gifted to young adults from all over the world.
